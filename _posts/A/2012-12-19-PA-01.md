>***Bounding summations***
>
>Give asymptotically tight bounds on the following summations. Assume that $$r \ge 0$$ and $$s \ge 0$$ are constants.
>
>1. $$\sum_{k = 1}^n k^r$$.
<br/>
2. $$\sum_{k = 1}^n \lg^s k$$.
<br/>
3. $$\sum_{k = 1}^n k^r \lg^s k$$.
